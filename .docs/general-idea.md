## Story
A teacher owns a private lesson house. There are classes, students and schedule. This teacher usually write down everything manually. The schedule of students, the ones who is already paid or not, the ones that's missing the schedule. Manual writing takes so long, and looking for the ones already paid this month also took so long because it needs paper flipping so much.

This teacher want to easily look for whoever doesn't pay this month, and whoever doesn't complete the schedule this month.
